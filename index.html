A software engineer with an eye for design

Organized, detail-oriented and a problem solver. I am a software engineer who dreams of creating beautiful front-end web-apps using her knowledge in both front-end and back-end languages and frameworks.

Coming from a creative background, I have developed a strong foundation in art & music growing up. Since I was young, I was also facinated with computers and I studied them with my father. I self-taught HTML when I was in high-school and as a hobby, I built simple web pages and loved the web design elements.

My vast experience in administration, customer service/retail and ESL teaching proves that I am an avid learner and can assimilate easily in different situations. Outside of programming, I also enjoy playing music, dancing, singing, and exploring fashion trends. I am keen on learning new things, am always curious and work hard to improve tasks at hand and make them beautiful.
